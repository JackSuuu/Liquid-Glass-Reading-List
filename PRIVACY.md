# Privacy Policy for LiquidGlass Reading List

**Last Updated**: November 17, 2025

## Overview
LiquidGlass Reading List is a browser extension that helps you save and organize web pages you want to read later.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect any personal data.** This extension does not track, collect, transmit, or share any information about you or your browsing activity.

## Data Storage
- **What we store**: Only the URLs and page titles that you explicitly choose to save to your reading list.
- **Where it's stored**: All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's sync storage API.
- **Who has access**: Only you. Your reading list data is private and syncs across your devices through your Chrome account.
- **Third parties**: No data is sent to external servers, third parties, or the extension developer.

## Permissions Used

### Storage Permission
Used to save your reading list items locally on your device and sync them across your Chrome browsers when you're signed in.

### Tabs Permission
Used only to retrieve the current webpage's URL and title when you click the "Add Current Page" button. We do not access tabs in the background or monitor your browsing activity.

## Data Control
- You have full control over your data
- Delete individual items by clicking the "Delete" button
- Remove all data by uninstalling the extension
- No data remains after uninstallation
